# How Corpus Works

## The Problem: Data Without Direction

Your Apple Watch and iPhone collect an incredible amount of health data — heart rate, sleep stages, activity, respiratory rate, and more. But raw numbers don't tell you what to *do*. Should you push harder today or rest? Is your sleep actually recovering you? What habits are helping and which are holding you back?

Corpus answers those questions. It's a personal health coach that reads the data your devices already collect and turns it into clear metrics, personalized insights, and structured training plans — all without extra hardware.

## Your Daily Experience

**Morning.** You open Corpus and see your daily coaching comment — a few sentences about how you slept, where your body stands today, and what to focus on. A quick three-question check-in asks about yesterday's habits (caffeine, stress, how you slept) to sharpen future insights.

**Throughout the day.** Your dashboard shows six health metrics at a glance: Sleep, Readiness, Strain, Stress, Recovery, and Steps. Each one has a clear level — not a cryptic number — so you know exactly where you stand. If you have a smart scale, body composition data appears automatically too.

**Planning.** Your week view lays out a personalized training plan. Accept a proposed workout, swap it, mark a day as busy, or plan your own session. The plan adapts to your schedule, your recovery, and your goals.

**Logging.** Snap a photo of your meal, tap a quick tag, or jot a note. Corpus tracks your behaviours and — over time — discovers which habits actually move the needle on your sleep, recovery, and readiness.

**Coaching.** Need advice? Open the chat. Your AI coach has full context on your health data, your goals, your plan, and your recent history. Ask it anything — from "Should I run today?" to "Why have I been sleeping poorly this week?"

**After a workout.** Open any completed workout for detailed breakdowns — heart rate zones, pace splits, route maps, and an AI-generated insight about your session.

## Who It's For

Corpus is for anyone who wants to understand their body better:

- **Athletes** who want periodized training plans, strain management, and deep workout analysis.
- **People striving to become their best selves** — optimizing sleep, managing stress, and building sustainable habits.
- **Runners gearing up for a race** — structured weekly plans with pacing, load tracking, and recovery guidance.
- **Anyone curious about their body** — if you've ever wondered what your health data actually means, Corpus makes it clear and actionable.

## What Makes Corpus Different

**No extra hardware.** Works with the Apple Watch and iPhone you already own. No rings, straps, or sensors to buy or charge.

**Learns your body.** Corpus doesn't apply generic advice. It discovers your personal patterns — what affects *your* sleep, *your* recovery, *your* readiness — and coaches you accordingly.

**Privacy-first.** All raw health data stays on your device. Only a structured daily summary is sent for coaching — over an encrypted connection, with no raw biometrics included.

## Getting Started

1. **Download Corpus** from the App Store.
2. **Set your goals** — whether it's running a marathon, improving sleep, or just understanding your body.
3. **Grant health permissions** — Corpus explains exactly what each data type is used for.
4. **Wear your Apple Watch** — the more data it collects, the smarter Corpus gets.
5. **Check in daily** — a few seconds each morning builds the picture that powers your insights.

Within a few days you'll see your first metrics and coaching. Within two to three weeks, Corpus will start surfacing personalized insights about your habits and their effects. The longer you use it, the more it learns.
